Our Services

Comprehensive cleaning solutions tailored to your needs

Basic Cleaning

Basic Cleaning consists of: floor sweeping/ vacuuming, floor mopping, furniture and countertop dusting, dish washing, make bed, *bed sheets change, *bath and kitchen towels change.

*if available

Deep Cleaning

Deep Cleaning consists of: floor sweeping/ vacuuming, floor mopping, furniture and countertop dusting, dish washing, door and window cleaning, bathroom and kitchen tile degreasing, bathroom and kitchen extractors cleaning, disinfecting door handles and switches, fridge and oven cleaning, make bed, *bed sheets change, *bath and kitchen towels change.

*if available

Couch/ Sofa Cleaning

Deep cleaning for your couches and sofas, removing stains and refreshing fabrics.

Electrostatic Cleaning

Electrostatic cleaning uses a special sprayer to apply a positively charged disinfectant mist, causing the particles to repel each other and wrap around surfaces (even hidden ones) for comprehensive, efficient, 360-degree coverage, ensuring thorough disinfection of hard-to-reach areas and complex objects in places like healthcare, schools, and offices. It's a post-manual cleaning step, not for removing dirt, that saves time and disinfectant by leaving an even coating without wiping.

Mattress Cleaning

Thorough mattress sanitization and stain remover for a healthier sleep environment.

Pool Cleaning

Our Pool cleaning takes care of particles and stains along the waterline the pool wall and floor.

Office Cleaning

Office Cleaning consits of: floor sweeping/ vacuuming, floor mopping, furniture, desktop and countertop dusting, dishwashing, door and window cleaning (inside), *restroom and pantry towel change, restroom and pantry extractor cleaning

* if available

Glass Wall Cleaning

Professional cleaning for large glass walls surfaces. We remove fingerprints, smudges, dust, and streaks to restore crystal clear transparency and enhance the appearance of your space.
